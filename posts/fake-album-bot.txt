# randomly created album covers
A bot for [r/fakealbumcovers](https://www.reddit.com/r/fakealbumcovers/). Needs some work. Particularly the fonts and their placement. Fork on [github](https://github.com/karlpokus/fakealbumcover-bot).

the Rules
- band name from https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special:Random
- album title from https://en.wikiquote.org/wiki/Special:Random
- 5th img from https://www.flickr.com/explore/interesting/7days/
